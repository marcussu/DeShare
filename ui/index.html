A-Share Mutual Funds

Platform based on Binance Smart Chain to enable decentralized mutual funds to be launched that are more transparent and cost-effective than traditional mutual funds. A-Share will also form a "family of mutual funds" to enable participants more flexibility to switch between funds.

Phase 1

USDT / BNB

  • Anyone can launch fund.
  • Shares issued as BEP20 tokens.
  • Buy shares with USDT.
  • Fees charged in BNB.

Phase 2

BUSD / ASHR

  • Smart contract driven fund.
  • Improved A-Share tokens utility.
  • Buy shares with USDT/BUSD.
  • Fees charged in BNB/ASHR.

Phase 3

TBC / TBC
