DeShare Mutual Funds

Platform based on Binance Smart Chain to enable decentralized mutual funds to be launched that are more transparent and cost-effective than traditional mutual funds. DeShare will also form a "family of mutual funds" to enable participants more flexibility to switch between funds.

DeShare Roadmap

A phased approach to the development of the platform provides a quick showcase of decentralized mutual funds while allowing room to scale and fine-tune along the way.

Phase 1

USDT / BNB

  • Anyone can launch fund.
  • Shares issued as BEP20 tokens.
  • Buy shares with USDT.
  • Fees charged in BNB.

Phase 2

BUSD / DSHR

  • Enhance decentralized governance.
  • Stake shares for DeShare tokens.
  • Improve DeShare tokens utility.
  • Add support for BUSD.

Phase 3

TBC / TBC
